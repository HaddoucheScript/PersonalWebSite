Bienvenue dans la page personnelle du Dr A. HADDOUCHE

I'm a teacher and researcher in physics. I taught general physics, electromagnetism and optics, algorithms and TCE (expression techniques in french). My field of research was the 'nuclear structure' . Since about 6 years, I changed my field of interest and I'm now working on quantum supersymmetry.
In addition to physics, I'm fascinated by many other fields including philosophy and hobbies like martial arts. I'm working as a teacher at the National School of Built and Ground Works Inginering in Algiers and as member of Theoretical Physics at USTHB university, Algiers.

Where I studied since my childhood?

My first day of school was in September 1985 (I was just 5 years old) at Moufdi Zakaria primary school in Ben Choubene, Rouiba (Algiers). I studied after that at Ahmed Aroua middle school (in the same location). Finally I went to Abdeslam Habachi and then Abdelmoumen High schools in Rouiba. After having the Baccalaurius, I enrolled at Houari Boumédien University of Science and Technology in Bab Ezzouar (Algiers), where I studied physics.
